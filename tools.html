Tools, Research and Advocacy Areas

The open source metadata reporting ISA software suite (http://isa-tools.org) embedded in over 30 public resources; institute-based, and global repositories, as well as data-driven journals: http://www.isacommons.org.

The informative and educational service for standards, repositories, and data policies, FAIRsharing (https://fairsharing.org), endorsed by a growing number of communities, including publishers, standardization groups, research data management support initiatives, and libraries.

A general-purpose STATistics Ontology (http://stato-ontology.org) to provide coverage for processes such as statistical tests and their conditions of application.
